Checkpoint A.3.5.

  1. Let \(I_n=\int\frac1{(1+x^2)^n}\,\operatorname{d}\!{x}\text{,}\) where \(n\) is a natural number. Use integration by parts to show that
    \begin{equation*} I_n = \frac{x}{(1+x^2)^n}+2n\,(I_n-I_{n+1}) \end{equation*}
    and hence establish the reduction formula
    \begin{equation*} 2n\,I_{n+1} = \frac{x}{(1+x^2)^n}+(2n-1)\,I_n \end{equation*}
    for \(n\ge 1\text{.}\) Hence find \(I_2\text{.}\)
  2. Evaluate \(\int\frac{1}{(x^2+4x+5)^2}\,\operatorname{d}\!{x}\text{.}\)
